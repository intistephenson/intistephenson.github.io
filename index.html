About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student at The George Washington University with a focus on how analytical design translates into the physical world.

My background includes hands-on experience in managing precise material standards and coordinating technical specifications as a Project Site Manager. I have a proven track record of leading both hardware and software development for competitive Vex Robotics teams, where I designed high-efficiency autonomous systems.

Beyond modeling complex **four-cylinder engines in SolidWorks** or programming drivetrains in **Python**, I am an active leader within the **Society of Professional Hispanic Engineers** and several other student organizations. I thrive in collaborative, high-pressure environments where solving problems requires both analytical rigor and effective communication.
